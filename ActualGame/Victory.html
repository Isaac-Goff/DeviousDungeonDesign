You rember.  You forgor but you rember.  You are not an 
adventurer from the kingdom of Enduria, you are the bane 
of the kingdom.  You are the great dungeon master of many 
dungeons just like this one spread around the kingdom.  
You have built many a dungeon like this both to store 
pillaged treasures and peoples, but also to lure foolhardy 
adventurers and remove their righteous genes from the gene 
pool.  A master plan, but you never thought that you may 
forgor these facts.  As you remember, you feel a sharp pain 
in your kidneys.  Someone has followed you in, someone who 
knows what you are.  As you look at the adventurer who 
stabbed you in shock.  As all fades to black, your many 
dungeons begin to collapse from the lack of your powerful 
magic.  The adventurer who stabbed you ran as soon as you 
saw him, but couldn't make it in time.  His sacrifice is 
remembered, as he saved a great many adventurers from 
the fate of being trapped or worse within your deviously 
designed dungeons.
