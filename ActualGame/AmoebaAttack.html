them whispering about a treasure not far into the dungeon.  It seems that 
they want it for themselves!  Before you can stop them from completing their  
plan, one of them shouts "They're onto us!" and pulls out a boomerang.  The 
impact to your head from the absurdly tiny boomerang was stronger than you could have 
expected, and the boomerang pierces your skull and ravages your brain.
