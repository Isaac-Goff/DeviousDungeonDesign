            seem to see.  Dragging you along, it moves into the wall of the dungeon.  
            It lets you go somewhere along the way, leaving you trapped inside the wall.                             
            Despite this, you don't seem to be suffocating.  As time passes inside the 
            blackness of the solid space you are stuck in, you realize in horror that 
            you are not dying.  You are trapped in the wall for eternety.
